_90-second revision_

|---|---|---|---|
| 200 | Overall Objectives | Reasonable assurance · skepticism | ⚠ Inherent limitations |

## Must-remember keywords
- **200:** reasonable assurance · skepticism · judgment · premise.
- **210:** preconditions · acceptable FRF · engagement letter · change in terms.
- **230:** NTE · experienced auditor · 60-day · 7-yr · auditor's property.
- **240:** fraudulent FR vs misappropriation · revenue presumption · override (JEs · estimates · unusual txns) · ADT-4.
- **250:** direct (SAAE) vs other (specified procs) · NOCLAR · escalate · modify.
- **260:** scope & timing · significant findings · independence (listed).
- **265:** significant deficiency · written · TCWG & mgmt.
- **299:** joint & several (common) · several (divided) · dissent → separate.

## 5 exam triggers
1. "Engagement oral; mgmt refuses IC duty" → **SA 210**.
2. "Year-end JEs shifting revenue" → **SA 240** + s.143(12).
3. "Licence cancelled, not disclosed" → **SA 250**.
4. "Post-signing procedures, no documented reason" → **SA 230**.
5. "Joint auditors divide orally; one hides fraud" → **SA 299**.

## Reporting bottom-line
- **200** → opinion: FS per FRF materially.
- **210** → no engagement without written terms + premise.
- **230** → documentation = SA compliance; retain 7 yrs.
- **240** → ≥ ₹1 cr → ADT-4/CG; else Board/AC.
- **250** → material NOCLAR unresolved → modify.
- **260/265** → significant findings/deficiencies in writing to TCWG.
- **299** → joint & several (common); dissent → separate.
