Must-remember keywords

  • 200: reasonable assurance, skepticism, SAAE
  • 210: preconditions, FRF, engagement letter
  • 230: experienced auditor, 60-day, 7-yr
  • 240: fraud triangle, revenue, override, s.143(12)
  • 250: direct, other, inquire, legal advice
  • 260: scope, findings, independence, two-way
  • 265: significant deficiency, written, TCWG
  • 299: joint plan, allocation, several, OM
  • 402: user/service, Type 1/2, CUECs

5 Exam Triggers

  1. MD blocks communication of audit plan / significant risks to Audit Committee → SA 260.
  2. Off-book sale; proceeds to CFO's personal account; mgmt pleads immateriality → SA 240 + s.143(12).
  3. DGGI/IRDAI notices for fictitious invoicing; mgmt withholds info → SA 250 → SA 705.
  4. Joint-auditor team fails on a common area (IT controls) → SA 299 joint & several.
  5. Joint auditors disagree on modification → SA 299 separate report + SA 706 OM.

Reporting bottom-lines

  • 200: opinion on FS/FRF.
  • 210: accept if preconditions met.
  • 230: supports opinion; no tampering.
  • 240: SA 705; fraud >₹1 cr → CG.
  • 250: NOCLAR → qualified; no SAAE → disclaimer.
  • 260: findings & independence.
  • 265: written to TCWG; opinion unchanged.
  • 299: common report; else separate+OM.
  • 402: no reference unless required.
