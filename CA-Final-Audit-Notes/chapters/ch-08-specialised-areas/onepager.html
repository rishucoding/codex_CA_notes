Must-remember keywords

  • 800: specific users, contract, cash basis, EoM, restrict use
  • 805: single FS, element, practicable, adapt SAs, separate opinion
  • 810: same auditor, applied criteria, consistent, fair summary, SA 560
  • Cross-cut: SA 210 FRF · SA 320 materiality · SA 700/705/706/720/701 reporting

Reporting bottom-lines

  • 800: unmodified per SA 700 + mandatory EoM: basis of accounting + (optional) distribution restriction.
  • 805: separate opinion; adverse/disclaimer on full FS blocks unmodified on single FS; element OK only if not a major portion + separate report + not prohibited by law.
  • 810: "consistent" or "fair summary"; mirror modifications/EoM/OM/MURGC from audited-FS report; adverse/disclaimer on audited FS → inappropriate to express opinion on summary FS.

5 Exam Triggers

  1. FS per loan agreement / bond indenture → SA 800: EoM alert + optional distribution restriction.
  2. Adverse on full FS; single cash-flow-stmt audit offered → SA 805: no unmodified on single FS (major portion rule).
  3. Audit of trade payables only while complete FS audited by another CA → SA 805: practicability + lower materiality.
  4. Mutual-fund abridged FS per SEBI format → SA 810: "fair summary" opinion; applied criteria presumed acceptable.
  5. Qualified opinion on audited FS; summary-FS report requested → SA 810: state + describe qualification & effect; if adverse/disclaimer → cannot express opinion on summary FS.
