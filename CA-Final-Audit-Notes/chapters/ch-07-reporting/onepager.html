Must-remember keywords

  • 700: reasonable assurance · 14 report elements · UDIN · personal + firm name
  • 701: most significance · TCWG-selected · listed mandatory · not a substitute
  • 705: "except for" · "do not present fairly" · "do not express an opinion" · pervasive = unconfined / substantial / fundamental
  • 706: EoM = IN FS, fundamental · OM = NOT in FS, audit-relevant · opinion unmodified
  • CARO: excludes banking · insurance · s.8 · OPC · small · specified pvt ltd

5 Exam Triggers

  1. Unverifiable debtors ₹150 cr + 40% inventory unverified → SA 705 Disclaimer; no KAM.
  2. Going-concern disclosure missing + bankruptcy likely → SA 705 Adverse.
  3. Missing ₹55 lakh loan agreement → SA 705 Qualified ("possible effects"); not EoM.
  4. Complex revenue recognition, listed co. → SA 701 KAM: describe & how addressed.
  5. Thermal project in Note 27 + branch FS by branch auditors → SA 706: EoM + OM; "opinion not modified".

Reporting bottom-lines

  • 700: written report · 14 elements · UDIN · date ≥ SAAE date.
  • 701: describe matter · why KAM · how addressed; no separate opinion.
  • 705: amend heading, Basis, SAAE statement; communicate with TCWG.
  • 706: separate section; state opinion unchanged; skip if SA 705/701 applies.
  • CARO: goes under "Report on Other Legal and Regulatory Requirements".
