Must-remember keywords

  • SQC 1: 6 elements · CEO ultimate · 60-day file · 7-yr retention · EQCR (listed) · rotation ≤ 7 yr
  • SA 220: premised on SQC 1 · sine qua non · EP overall quality · report not dated till EQCR done
  • EQCR: partner / ICAI member / external · objective · no participation
  • Red flags: ED/IT search · previous auditor resigned · fee hike · family link · aggressive accounting
  • Oversight: PRB (ICAI) · QRB (non-NFRA) · NFRA (listed/bank/insurer/Rule 3)

5 Exam Triggers

  1. MP's family friend is promoter; prev. auditor resigned; 25% fee hike → SQC 1 Acceptance & Leadership.
  2. Paid-employee CA solicits statutory audit during stock audit + clean-report quid pro quo → SQC 1 Ethics.
  3. Listed-co report dated 15 May; EQCR completed 18 May → SA 220: report not dated before EQCR complete.
  4. EQCR disagrees with EP on revenue / onerous contracts → SQC 1 & SA 220: resolve before report; escalate.
  5. CARO vii(a) "generally regular" for listed co. → SA 220: fails regulatory-compliance objective.

Reporting bottom-lines

  • SQC 1: firm system; documented & communicated.
  • SA 220: listed-entity report not dated until EQCR complete.
  • Rotation: listed-entity EP ≤ 7 yrs (sole-practitioner → peer review).
  • Files: assemble ≤ 60 days · retain ≥ 7 yrs from report date.
