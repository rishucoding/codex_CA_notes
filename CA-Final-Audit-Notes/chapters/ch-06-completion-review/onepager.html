Must-remember keywords

  • 710: corresponding figures · comparative FS · predecessor · Other Matter · opening balances
  • 720: annual report · OI · material inconsistency · OI section · listed (obtained/expected) · nothing-to-report vs MM
  • 560: W-1 active · W-2/3 passive · new / dual-dated · EOM restricted
  • 570: material uncertainty · MURGC section · cash-flow forecast · mitigating factors
  • 580: necessary-not-sufficient · as-near-as-practicable · all periods · SA 705 if refused
  • 450 / 260: factual / judgemental / projected · TCWG 2-way · independence (listed)

5 Exam Triggers

  1. Prior-period qualification unresolved, CF approach → SA 710 — modify + Other Matter para.
  2. Chairman's report shows different profit than FS; refusal to correct → SA 720 — describe uncorrected MM of OI; consider withdrawal.
  3. MRL three-and-half months before report; material acquisition in gap → SA 580 + 560 — refresh rep; extend subsequent-event work.
  4. CFS approach — current opinion on prior differs from earlier → SA 710 — Other Matter disclosing reasons.
  5. Civil suit lost after report but before FS issued; FS amended → SA 560 W-2 — new / dual-dated report not earlier than approval of amended FS.

Reporting bottom-lines

  • 710 CF: silent unless triggered · CFS: opinion each period · predecessor/unaudited → OM.
  • 720: separate OI section — "nothing to report" or describe uncorrected MM; opinion excludes OI.
  • 560: adjust / disclose; new or dual-dated report post-report-date.
  • 570: inappropriate → adverse; uncertainty + adequate disclosure → unmodified + MURGC; inadequate → qual / adverse.
  • 580: refusal / unreliable → SA 705, often disclaimer.
  • 450 / 260: communicate uncorrected MS & expected modifications to TCWG pre-sign.
