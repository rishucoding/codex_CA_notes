5 Exam Triggers

  1. Picks only > ₹25 lacs items by judgment → SA 500/530 — selective exam not sampling; no projection.
  2. NH-7 blockade blocks year-end count → SA 501 — unforeseen, not impracticable; alt-date + intervening procedures.
  3. 25/30 creditors' GST cancelled but confirmations received → SA 505/500 — responses unreliable; fraud-risk; extend N/T/E.
  4. Initial audit; prior unaudited; material opening inventory → SA 510 — observe current count + reconcile + valuation/GP/cut-off; else qualified/disclaimer.
  5. MD pays ₹20 cr to shell, infuses ₹5 cr equity → SA 550 + SA 240 — undisclosed RP; MCA/MBP-1; fraud procedures; SA 580 WR.

Reporting bottom-lines

  • 500: no SAAE → qualified/disclaimer.
  • 501: no alt → scope-limit; counsel refuses → modify.
  • 505: non-response + alt fails → modify; mgmt-refusal → TCWG.
  • 510: opening issue → qualified/adverse; predecessor mod survives.
  • 530: no reasonable basis → extend / alt control / modify.
  • 550: undisclosed RP / non-arm's-length → modify; fraud → SA 240.
  • 560: Type I adjust; Type II disclose; won't revise → prevent reliance.
  • 570: unmodified · unmodified+MU · qualified/adverse · adverse.
  • 580: refusal/unreliable → disclaimer.
