Must-remember keywords

  • 4400: audit-nature procedures, factual findings, no assurance, restricted, 15 report elements
  • 4410: historical FI, applicable FRF, no verify, no opinion/conclusion, SQC 1
  • Both: non-assurance, engagement letter, Code of Ethics, documentation

Report language (memorise)

  • 4400: "procedures do not constitute an audit or review and, as such, no assurance is expressed"; "report is restricted"; "does not extend to FS taken as a whole".
  • 4410: "compiled the FI based on information provided by management"; "does not express an audit opinion or a review conclusion".

5 Exam Triggers

  1. Confirm AR balances >₹10 L; non-statutory CA → SRS 4400 report elements.
  2. Client gives TB, wants FS prepared + report → SRS 4410; no verification.
  3. Compilation: inventory understated; mgmt refuses correction → withdraw; integrity.
  4. AUP distribution restriction conflicts with law → do not accept.
  5. Compilation FI otherwise misleading (Rourkela-unit omission) → propose amendments; SQC 1 applies, SA 220 does not.

Reporting bottom-lines

  • 4400: factual findings only · restricted · 15 elements · "no assurance".
  • 4410: compilation report · 13 elements · no opinion/conclusion · mgmt owns FI.
