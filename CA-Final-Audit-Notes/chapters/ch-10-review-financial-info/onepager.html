Ch 10 — Review of Financial Information · One-Pager

ICAI SM Final P3 May 2026 · ~6–8 marks · SRE 2400 · SRE 2410

SREs at a glance

SRENameCore trigger⚠ Key exception
2400Review of historical FSPractitioner NOT the auditor · limited assurance · inquiry + analyticalNo rational purpose / integrity doubt / scope limit → do not accept
2410Review of interim FIEntity's own auditor · SEBI LODR quarterly review · update prior understandingOrdinarily no tests of records, no lawyer letter, no post-report procedures

Must-remember keywords

  • 2400: limited assurance, preconditions, inquiry+analytical, additional procedures, written reps, disclaim/withdraw
  • 2410: interim FI (shorter than year), update understanding, moderate risk, EOM for going concern, UDIN, resign from annual audit
  • Audit vs Review: reasonable vs limited · opinion vs conclusion · positive vs negative wording · SAAE all vs inquiry+analytical

ICAI negative-assurance phrase

"Based on our review, nothing has come to our attention that causes us to believe that the financial statements do not give a true and fair view (or do not present fairly, in all material respects), in accordance with the applicable financial reporting framework."

5 Exam Triggers

  1. Contract requires quarterly review; practitioner not the auditor → SRE 2400 (special purpose FRF ok).
  2. Statutory auditor doing SEBI LODR quarterly review → SRE 2410; UDIN mandatory.
  3. Suspected misstatement during review → additional procedures (detail tests / confirmations); else scope limit → qualified / disclaimer.
  4. Fire, uninsured assets, going-concern uncertainty disclosed in interim FI → SRE 2410 EOM; if not disclosed → qualified / adverse.
  5. Material adjustment needed; CFO & AC silent → SRE 2410: modify report / withdraw / resign from annual audit.

Reporting bottom-lines

  • Unmodified (2400/2410): "nothing has come to our attention…" per FRF.
  • Qualified: "except for…" — material, not pervasive.
  • Adverse: "due to the significance of…" — material & pervasive misstatement.
  • Disclaimer: unable to obtain SAAE — material & pervasive.
  • EOM: going-concern uncertainty adequately disclosed.
  • 2410 extras: membership no. + FRN + UDIN + place.
