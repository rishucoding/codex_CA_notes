## Logging Rule

Record an item here when:

- the source wording matters more than the paraphrase,
- a rule may have changed since the material was published,
- the answer depends on a specific amendment, notification, or ICAI update,
- the chapter worker is uncertain whether a line is conceptual or source-specific.

## Working Note

Keep this list practical. It is not a place for every minor editorial doubt. It is for things that can change the answer, the wording, or the exam relevance.
